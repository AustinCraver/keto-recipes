Keto Tacos

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves: 3-4 (Unless you're really hungry!)
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce (Local and Organic) One head
Ground Beef (Grass Fed) One Pound
Cheese (Real Cheese) 2 Cups
Onion (Local and Organic) Half of a large onion

Step 1: Brown the beef until it is thoroughly cooked.

Step 2: Shred or cut up the cheese.

Step 3: Tear lettuce into large leaves.

Step 4: Dice the onion into small pieces.

Step 5: Combine ingredients like you would with a taco, and enjoy!


Keto Breakfast

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves:1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 4
Bacon 16 strips
Cheese (Real Cheese) 1/2 Cup

Step 1: Cook the bacon to your desired level of crispiness.

Step 2: Shred or cut up the cheese while the bacon is cooking.

Step 3: Cook the eggs (in the bacon grease) to your liking.

Step 4: Plate and enjoy!


Keto Dessert

  • Time: 5 Minutes
  • Serves: 3-4 (Unless you have quite the sweet tooth!)
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar Free Pudding Mix One Box
Heavy Whipping Cream Two Cups

Step 1: Pour the pudding mix into a large bowl.

Step 2: Add two cups of heavy whipping cream.

Step 3: Whisk until the sweet dessert takes on the consistency of whipped cream. An electric mixer will save you tons of time and energy.

Step 4: Chill it in the fridge if you want it extra cold.

Step 5: Otherwise, put some in a small bowl or coffee cup, grab a spoon, and enjoy!
